Below are some tips on how best to use this Atlas of the Maldives. If you know which part of the Maldives you would like to see, simply click on the 'Maldives Map' button and then select the appropriate number of the atoll you want to see in more detail.You can find the meaning of the symbols on the detailed maps by clicking on the ‘Legend’ button.   If you purchased the interactive ebook version, then the atoll maps will show more features.  If you have any suggestions or questions, or just want to say “Hi” feel free to send us a message.
